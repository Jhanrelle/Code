About Us

Welcome to EcoBean Café, where every cup of coffee is delicious. We source our beans from organic, fair-trade farms and use eco-friendly practices like compostable cups and energy-efficient appliances. Our café is a hub for sustainability-minded individuals, featuring workshops and events that promote environmental action. Enjoy our fresh, artisanal coffee and be part of a community that cares about both quality and the planet.

EcoBean Cafe started as a small cafe selling in streets, until build own shop in the city. Pet-friendly shop, aiming to serve up delicious coffee and pastries. It quickly became a favorite spot for students and locals.
